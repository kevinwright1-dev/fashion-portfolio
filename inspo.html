Moodboard / Reference Point

Inspo

This page is the blueprint behind the fits. It is less about copying looks and more about pulling energy, silhouettes, attitude, and details from people who shaped how I see fashion.

People On The Board 10 Music, style, internet, and pop culture all mixed together.
Main Themes Risk, texture, confidence The fits that stick usually feel lived-in, sharp, and a little unpredictable.
How I Use It Build, remix, wear Take one detail, one silhouette, or one attitude and make it my own.
